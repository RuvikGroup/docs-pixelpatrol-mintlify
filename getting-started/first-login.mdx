# Welcome to PixelPatrol!

This guide will walk you through your first login and help you get oriented with the PixelPatrol dashboard.

## Step 1: Access PixelPatrol

1. Open your web browser and navigate to your PixelPatrol instance
2. You'll see the login page with options to sign in

![Login Page](screenshots/login-page.png)

## Step 2: Choose Your Login Method

PixelPatrol supports multiple authentication methods:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Email Login">
    ### Sign in with Email
    
    1. Enter your email address
    2. Enter your password
    3. Click "Sign In"
    
    <Note>
    If you forgot your password, click "Forgot Password?" to receive a reset link via email.
    </Note>
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Magic Link">
    ### Sign in with Magic Link
    
    1. Enter your email address
    2. Click "Send Magic Link"
    3. Check your email for the login link
    4. Click the link in your email to sign in automatically
    
    <Info>
    Magic links expire after 1 hour for security reasons.
    </Info>
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="OAuth">
    ### Sign in with OAuth
    
    If your administrator has configured OAuth providers, you can sign in using:
    - Google
    - GitHub
    - Other configured providers
    
    Simply click the appropriate button to authenticate.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Step 3: First-Time Setup

If this is your first time logging in, you'll need to complete a few setup steps:

### Create or Join a Team

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Creating a New Team">
    If you're the first person from your organization:
    
    1. Click "Create New Team"
    2. Enter your team name
    3. Click "Create Team"
    
    You'll automatically become the team owner with full administrative privileges.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Joining an Existing Team">
    If you've been invited to join a team:
    
    1. Check your email for an invitation
    2. Click the invitation link
    3. You'll be automatically added to the team
    
    Your permissions will be based on the role assigned by the team administrator.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Step 5: Complete Your Profile

Before diving into moderation setup:

1. Click your profile icon in the top-right corner
2. Select "Profile Settings"
3. Complete your profile information:
   - Display name
   - Profile picture (optional)
   - Notification preferences

## Troubleshooting Login Issues

<Warning>
Having trouble logging in? Try these solutions:
</Warning>

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| "Invalid credentials" error | Double-check your email and password. Passwords are case-sensitive. |
| Magic link expired | Request a new magic link. They expire after 1 hour. |
| "Email not verified" message | Check your email for a verification link and click it. |
| Can't receive emails | Check your spam folder or contact your IT administrator. |
